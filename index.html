Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a booking?

+

You can book by calling us directly, visiting our facility, or messaging us on Instagram @noxentertainmentsystem. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability!

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept Cash and UPI payments. All sessions must be paid in advance before starting your gaming experience.

Can I bring my own games?

+

Our library includes all PS Plus and Xbox Game Pass Ultimate titles. You're welcome to bring your own physical games for PS5, but we have an extensive collection available!

What's included in the VR experience?

+

Our VR package includes premium VR headsets with motion controllers, access to popular VR titles, and a safe, designated play area. Our staff will guide you through setup and safety protocols.

Is there an age limit?

+

Players aged 7+ are welcome! Age-appropriate content restrictions apply: Ages 7-12 can play E and E10+ rated games. Ages 13-17 can play up to Teen rated games (Mature games require parental presence and waiver). 18+ have unrestricted access with valid ID.

Can I book for a group or party?

+

Absolutely! We offer multiplayer sessions for 2-4 players. Group bookings and birthday parties are welcome - contact us for special arrangements and pricing.

What if equipment malfunctions during my session?

+

If there's a technical issue on our end (console freeze, power outage, ISP failure), we'll either extend your session for the lost time or provide a refund. Issues outside our control (game updates you selected, low controller battery you didn't check) are not covered.

Do you have food and drinks available?

+

Food and beverages are strictly prohibited near gaming equipment to protect our hardware. We have a designated area where you can take breaks and enjoy refreshments.

What's your cancellation policy?

+

Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel. We'll work with you on rescheduling based on availability. Our priority is ensuring everyone gets fair access to our gaming stations.

How do I get on the F1 25 leaderboard?

+

Set your best lap time on our sim racing setup! Our staff will verify and record top times. Only legitimate, verified runs are added to the leaderboard.
